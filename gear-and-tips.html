Fishing Gear & Tips

Ready to wet a line? Here's what you need to get started fishing, plus some helpful tips.

Essential Gear

  • Rod and reel
  • Fishing line, assorted hooks
  • Bobbers or weights

Fishing Tips

  • Use live bait for better luck
  • Find structure where fish hide
  • Learn how to read the water
